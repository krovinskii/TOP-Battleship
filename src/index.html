BattleShip
  1. Step 1: Enter Player Names & Select Opponent Type
    • Player 1 enters their name.
    • Player 2's type is selected: either "Human" or "AI".
  2. Step 2: Press Play
    • After entering player names and selecting the opponent type, the user presses the “Play” button to start the game.
  3. Step 3: Place Ships on the Grid
    • Each player places their ships on their gameboard. The ships can be placed either manually by dragging them onto the grid or automatically with random placement.
  4. Step 4: Start the Game
    • The game begins with Player 1 taking the first turn. Player 1 selects a coordinate on the opponent's grid to attack.
    • The opponent (either AI or Player 2) responds based on their turn.
  5. Step 5: Track Hits and Misses
    • Players take turns attacking each other's grid. If a ship is hit, the grid will update to reflect the hit. Misses will be marked on the grid as well.
  6. Step 6: Check for Sunk Ships
    • As the game progresses, players will check if they've sunk any ships. A ship is considered sunk when all its segments are hit. The game will notify the player when this happens.
  7. Step 7: Declare the Winner
    • The game ends when one player successfully sinks all of their opponent's ships. The winner is declared, and the player is given an option to restart or end the game.
